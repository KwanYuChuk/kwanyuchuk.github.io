The main datasets considered include distance from state roads, geomorphic wetlands, wetlands, vegetation, soil types and land use. Two locations were identified as the most favourable translocation sites because they meet habitat requirements and have sufficient area Julimar State Forest and Gingilup Swamps Nature Reserve.
