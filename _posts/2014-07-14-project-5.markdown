description: Ashfield Flats Reserve is the largest existing river-flat in Perth Metropolitan city. It is listed as a threated ecological community under Environmental Protection and Biodiversity Conservation. Ashfield flat is a habitat to many halophytes (D. G. McGrath, n.d.). However, this reserve faces threats from various pollutants in sediment and water. These pollutants have significant impacts on flora and fauna in Ashfield Flats. This study (1) Investigated the level and distribution of potential contaminants from sediment and water, evaluate contaminants with Australian guidelines; and  (2) Examined possible sources of contaminants, taking into consideration the surrounding environment and historical factors.
